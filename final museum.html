National Museum

The National Museum in Delhi showcases a vast collection of art and artifacts from Indian history, including sculptures, paintings, manuscripts, and textiles.

National Gallery of Modern Art (NGMA)

The NGMA houses modern and contemporary Indian art and includes works by renowned artists, exhibitions, and cultural programs.

National Rail Museum

The National Rail Museum in Delhi offers a fascinating journey through the history of Indian Railways, featuring vintage locomotives, models, and exhibits.

Gandhi Smriti

Gandhi Smriti is a museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi, featuring his life, teachings, and the spot where he was assassinated.

National Crafts Museum

The Crafts Museum displays India's rich tradition of handicrafts, with live craft demonstrations, textiles, and folk art.

Indira Gandhi Memorial Museum

The museum is dedicated to Indira Gandhi, showcasing her personal belongings, photographs, and the events of her life and legacy.

Indian Air Force Museum

The Indian Air Force Museum highlights the history and achievements of the Indian Air Force, featuring aircraft, uniforms, and memorabilia.

Shankar's International Dolls Museum

The Dolls Museum features an extensive collection of dolls from around the world, showcasing cultural diversity and craftsmanship.

FAQs

What are the visiting hours for the National Museum?

The National Museum is open from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM every day except on public holidays.

Is there free entry to any museums?

Yes, the Gandhi Smriti Museum and Sulabh International Museum of Toilets offer free entry to visitors.

Are there any museums suitable for children?

The Shankar's International Doll Museum and Nehru Planetarium are great for children, offering engaging and interactive exhibits.

Do museums in Delhi offer guided tours?

Many museums, like the National Museum and Rail Museum, provide guided tours at an additional cost.

What are the ticket prices for foreign tourists?

The National Museum charges ₹650 for foreign tourists, while the Handicrafts Museum charges ₹200.

Are there food facilities near the museums?

Most museums in Delhi have nearby food stalls or cafes, and some, like the Rail Museum, have in-house cafeterias.
