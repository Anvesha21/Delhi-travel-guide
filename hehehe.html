Akshardham Temple

Akshardham Temple is a spiritual-cultural campus in Delhi. It is dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan and depicts the traditional art and cultural heritage of India.

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple, shaped like a lotus flower, is a Bahá'í House of Worship. It is a prominent tourist attraction and is known for its peaceful ambiance.

ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple is a beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and is known for its vibrant community, spiritual teachings, and cultural events.

Hanuman Temple

The Hanuman Temple in Connaught Place is dedicated to Lord Hanuman, and is one of the oldest and most revered temples in Delhi.

Kalkaji Mandir

Kalkaji Mandir is dedicated to Goddess Kali. It is a major temple located in South Delhi, known for its spiritual significance and vibrant rituals.

Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir is a beautiful Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi, located in Delhi. The temple is known for its architectural beauty.

Jhandewalan

Jhandewalan Temple is a Hindu temple near Karol Bagh in Delhi, India dedicated to the goddess Vaishno Devi.[1] It is among the oldest temple in Delhi and located on Jhandewala road

Chhatarpur Temple

Chhatarpur Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Katyayani. The entire complex of the temple is spread over a wide area of 28 hectares. It is located in Chhatarpur, on the southwestern outskirts of the New Delhi. It is the 2nd largest temple in Delhi, after the Akshardham Temple

Akshardham Temple, also known as the Akshardham Swaminarayan Temple, is a spiritual-cultural campus dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan. It is famous for its grandeur, the central monument, and the exhibitions depicting the rich heritage of India.

The Lotus Temple is renowned for its unique lotus flower-shaped design. It serves as a Bahá'í House of Worship and is open to people of all faiths. Its peaceful environment and stunning architecture make it a prominent landmark in Delhi.

The ISKCON Temple in Delhi was established in 1998. It is a major center of Lord Krishna worship and cultural activities. The temple is known for its vibrant atmosphere, prayers, and spiritual programs.

The Hanuman Temple in Connaught Place is one of the oldest and most revered temples in Delhi. It is famous for the large idol of Lord Hanuman and its historical significance in the city’s religious landscape.

Kalkaji Mandir, dedicated to Goddess Kali, is one of the major temples in South Delhi. Visitors should respect the religious rituals and be aware that it can get crowded during festivals. It opens early in the morning and closes late in the evening.

Birla Mandir is popular due to its magnificent architectural design and dedication to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi. The temple is famous for its serenity and beautiful surroundings, making it a must-visit for those interested in spirituality and architecture.
